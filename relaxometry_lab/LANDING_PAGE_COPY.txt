WORKFLOW SECTION ("Four steps from data to result")
----------------------------------------------------
[workflow.eyebrow]   Workflow
[workflow.title]     Four steps from data to result
[workflow.subtitle]  No scripts. No command line. Works with DICOM, NIfTI, and raw Bruker exports.

  Step 1 — Load Data
  Upload DICOM, NIfTI, or a Bruker study ZIP. Optionally add a segmentation mask.

  Step 2 — Preview
  Browse your scan in 3-plane orthographic view, scroll slices, zoom, and verify alignment.

  Step 3 — Fit
  Run voxel-wise curve fitting. Explore individual decay curves and ROI statistics.

  Step 4 — Export
  Download NIfTI maps, CSV statistics, a multi-page PDF report, or raw NumPy arrays.


DATA FORMATS SECTION
---------------------
[dataFormats.eyebrow]   Data Formats
[dataFormats.title]     Supported inputs
[dataFormats.subtitle]  Bring your data in any of these formats — no preprocessing needed.

  Card 1 — DICOM
  [desc]  Single enhanced multi-frame DICOM or a stack of per-slice single-frame files. Echo time read from standard tags.
  [tags]  .dcm

  Card 2 — NIfTI
  [desc]  One file per echo (3D) or a single 4D volume. Echo times read from BIDS sidecar JSON when present.
  [tags]  .nii, .nii.gz

  Card 3 — Bruker Study ZIP
  [desc]  Zip a Bruker ParaVision study folder and upload it directly. Browse all scans, pick one, and load with a click.
  [tags]  .zip, ParaVision
